How It Works

Three taps. Full conversation.
No interpreter needed.

01

Pick a phrase

200+ clinical phrases organized by visit phase. Intake, pain assessment, exam instructions, treatment, exercises, diagnosis. Search or browse.

02

Show your patient

The phrase appears in large text in their language with native audio playback. Hand them the phone. They read it, hear it, understand it.

03

Get their response

Your patient taps yes, no, or selects from options. You see the answer in English. The app suggests your next question. A real two-way conversation.
